Connect | Contribute | Enjoy

A one day event at IIT Jodhpur comprising of talks and hands-on-sessions to get people acquainted to Open-source software, spread the awareness and know about Mozilla and how to work on its platform.

Sessions

  • Introduction to FOSS
  • Brief Introduction to Mozilla
  • Linux Kernel Hacking
  • Mozilla Platform Products
  • What Can I Do For Mozilla
  • Hands on Session: Webmaker Tools
  • Hands on Session: Build your own Firefox
  • Firefox OS Demo

Register
